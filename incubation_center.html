About Us

At TBC, we believe entrepreneurship drives progress. That's why we provide emerging startups with everything they need to succeed: expert mentorship, comprehensive training programmes, collaborative workspaces, and strategic networking opportunities - all without taking any equity in your business. Your vision remains entirely yours. In everything we do, we encourage a sustainable and environmentally responsible approach to innovation. We actively support ideas that promote green growth, climate-conscious solutions, and technology-driven sustainability - empowering founders to build businesses that make a positive impact while carrying the 3Ps: People, Planet, and Profit.

Frequently Asked Questions

TBC Incubation Centre is open to students, alumni, and external entrepreneurs with innovative ideas and a passion for startups. Whether you're at the idea stage or already running a business, we provide tailored support to help you grow.

Students, alumni, and external entrepreneurs with innovative ideas and a passion for startups can benefit from our incubation centre. We welcome anyone committed to building successful ventures.

We offer mentorship programs, workspace facilities, networking opportunities, access to funding resources, business development support, and connections to industry experts and investors.

Our mentorship program connects you with experienced entrepreneurs and industry professionals who provide one-on-one guidance, strategic advice, and support throughout your startup journey.

We facilitate connections with angel investors, venture capitalists, and funding agencies. We also help prepare pitch decks and provide guidance on funding strategies and applications.

We support startups across various sectors including technology, healthcare, education, fintech, and social enterprises. We're open to innovative ideas that have potential for growth and impact.

The typical incubation period ranges from 6 months to 2 years, depending on your startup's needs and progress. We offer flexible programs tailored to your growth trajectory.

Our equity requirements vary based on the level of support and resources provided. We'll discuss this transparently during the application process to ensure mutual benefit.

We organize workshops, networking sessions, pitch competitions, masterclasses, and industry meetups. These events help entrepreneurs learn, connect, and grow their ventures.

You can register for our programs through our website or by contacting us directly. We announce upcoming events through our newsletter and social media channels.

Most of our events are free for incubated startups and members. Some specialized workshops or programs may have a nominal fee to cover costs.
